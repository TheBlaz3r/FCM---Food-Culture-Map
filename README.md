# FCM---Food-Culture-Map
Description:
This is the project for Apple Foundation Program of M8S team. This app will let you learn about the food culture of several countries, you can test your knowledge in an interactive way and also try the special feature "AI Classifier" to detect the food that you encounter.

Features:
Explore Tab -> You can discover new dishes by tapping on the pins of the globe
Game Tab -> You can test your knowledge trying to guess the location of the dishes
Detect Tab -> Here you can take a picture of a dish and the classifier will try to tell you its name
Liked -> Your favorite dishes will appear here
